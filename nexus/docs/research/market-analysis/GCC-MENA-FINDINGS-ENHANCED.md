## 4. ZATCA E-INVOICING COMPLIANCE

### Regulatory Timeline

| Wave | Revenue Threshold | Compliance Deadline |
|------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Phase 2 (ongoing) | SAR 70M+ | January 1, 2024 |
| Wave 11 | SAR 10-15M | December 1, 2024 |
| Wave 14 | SAR 5-7M | February 1, 2025 |
| Wave 22 | SAR 1M+ (2022-2024) | December 31, 2025 |
| Wave 24 | SAR 375K+ | June 30, 2026 |

### Technical Requirements
- **Real-Time Reporting (CTR):** Immediate transmission to ZATCA
- **Cryptographic Stamp (UUID):** Unique digital signature per invoice
- **B2B Processing:** Real-time clearance required
- **B2C Processing:** Report within 24 hours
- **Retention:** 5-year secure archival mandate

### Penalty Structure

| Violation | Fine Range |
|-----------|------------|
| Non-compliance with CTR | SAR 5,000 - 50,000 per violation |
| Missing QR code | Up to SAR 10,000 per invoice |
| Delayed integration | Additional penalties + VAT suspension risk |

### Pain Points
- Outdated legacy invoice systems
- Missing IT resources and personnel
- ERP/POS system integration complexity
- Multiple compliance requirements across GCC

### Nexus Workflow Opportunities
- "When invoice generated → Validate ZATCA format → Submit to Fatoorah → Confirm clearance"
- "When payment received → Match to invoice → Update ZATCA → Generate receipt"
- "When compliance deadline approaching → Audit status → Generate report → Alert accountant"

**Sources:** [ZATCA Official](https://zatca.gov.sa/en/E-Invoicing/Introduction/Pages/Roll-out-phases.aspx), [EY Tax Alerts](https://www.ey.com/en_gl/technical/tax-alerts/saudi-arabia-announces-23rd-wave-of-phase-2-e-invoicing-integration), [Wafeq](https://www.wafeq.com/en-sa/e-invoicing-in-saudi-arabia/preparing-for-e-invoicing/zatca-e-invoicing-phase-2)
