## QUESTIONNAIRE IMPLICATIONS

Based on individual-focused research, the questionnaire should:

### DO Use:
- "What task are YOU spending too much time on?"
- "What's frustrating YOU about running your business?"
- "How many hours do YOU personally waste on [task]?"
- "What would YOU do with an extra 10 hours/week?"

### DON'T Use:
- "Does your organization need workflow automation?"
- "What integration tools does your company use?"
- "How does your IT team handle automation?"
- "What's your enterprise automation budget?"

**Key Principle:** Speak to the PERSON, not the COMPANY. The individual feels the pain - that's who decides to buy.
