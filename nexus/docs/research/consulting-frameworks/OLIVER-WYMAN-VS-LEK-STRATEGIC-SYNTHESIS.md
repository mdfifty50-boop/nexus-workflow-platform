- **Oliver Wyman:** Financial engineering, risk quantification, scenario planning, pricing optimization

After systematic debate across 5 critical dimensions, this synthesis identifies **12 points of strong agreement** (high-confidence strategic signals), **5 significant disagreements** (requiring deeper resolution), and produces a **unified strategic recommendation** that captures the best of both perspectives while addressing their blind spots.
