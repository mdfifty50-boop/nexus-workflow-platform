# Kuwait SME Software Research Report

**Date:** January 2026
**Purpose:** Identify commonly used software by SMEs and entrepreneurs in Kuwait for Nexus integration priorities

---

## Executive Summary

Over 70% of Kuwaiti businesses have adopted digital solutions, with SMEs leading digital transformation. The Kuwait market shows strong preference for:
- **Arabic/English bilingual software**
- **KNET payment integration** (handles 80%+ of online transactions)
- **WhatsApp Business** as primary customer communication channel
- **Cloud-based, mobile-first solutions**

## Regional Preferences & Notes

### Language Requirements
- **Bilingual (Arabic/English) UI is essential** for adoption
- Right-to-left (RTL) text support required
- Many SME owners prefer Arabic-first interfaces

### Compliance Considerations
- Kuwait Labor Law compliance for HR/Payroll tools
- VAT reporting capabilities (5% VAT in GCC)
- KNET certification for payment processing
- Central Bank of Kuwait regulations for payment providers

### Mobile-First Market
- High smartphone penetration
- Mobile commerce dominates
- Apps preferred over web for many transactions

### Government Support
- National Fund for SMEs has invested $350M+ in 800+ startups
- Vision 2035 driving digital transformation
- Kuwait Business Center offers micro-business licenses

### Key Local Software Providers
- **AbeerTech** - Mobile apps, logistics software
- **Whizkey** - Custom software development
- **Uniweb IT Solutions** - Web/e-commerce
- **Poixel** - Web, mobile, digital marketing
